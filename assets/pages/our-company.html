Where We Started

PcTech is a computer retail company that was founded on the principles of convenience, accessibility, and exceptional customer service. Believe it or not, this local technology retail leader had humble beginnings, when five friends who were passionate about technology sought out secondhand PCs in the Tipid PC online group to resell at reasonable prices.
