Cancellation Policy

  1. Customers have the complete right to cancel their orders if they so desire. If the orders are still in the pending status, the option will appear in the "My Orders" section.
  2. Once an order has been marked as fulfilled, it can no longer be cancelled through the "My Orders" section. Customers who encounter this can contact PcTech via the PcTech website, Facebook page, or email salespctech@gmail.com.ph.
  3. Once an item has been shipped, it is no longer possible to cancel it. Delivery rescheduling can be arranged in exceptional circumstances. If the delivery needs to be rescheduled, customers should contact PcTech.
  4. Orders may be cancelled by PcTech due to the following reasons.
    1. The item/s turned out to be out of stock.
    2. Failed delivery
    3. Order is classified to be fraudulent.
    4. Other cases when orders cannot be fulfilled (i.e. out of stock items, preorder items no longer available)
